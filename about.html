About Me

Hello! My name is Alexander Wang. I'm a sophomore at Cornell University double majoring in applied mathematics and computer science.

Having witnessed the rise of artificial intelligence and machine learning in our daily lives, from social media recommendations to self-driving cars, I dream of a future where smart technology enable people to form a post-scarcity society. To that end, I've made it my academic goal to understand the foundations and applications of artificial intelligence to lead humanity towards an intelligently automated world.

On campus, I'm a part of the Insights subteam for the Cornell Data Science (CDS) project team. As a new member of this group, I await a project assignment. However, I was a consultant for a CDS Insights project focused on creating a machine learning model to lip read videos during the fall semester of 2020.

Aside from academics, I also enjoy sports, casual chess, and video games. I played badminton semi-professionally throughout my childhood and high school and still regularly go on runs.
